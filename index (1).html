About Me

Hi, I'm Alvi Yuana Febriyanti, a Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data analysis, predictive modeling, and data-driven decision-making. I have hands-on experience across government institutions and state-owned enterprises, working with data to support business, operational, and governance improvements.

My expertise includes Python, SQL, PostgreSQL, Power BI, Grafana, Streamlit, Flask, and Pentaho. I have experience building analytical dashboards, performing forecasting and financial analysis, developing ETL pipelines, and creating web-based data solutions.

I am also familiar with IT governance and data security practices, including COBIT, ISO 27001, and NIST. With an analytical mindset, attention to detail, and adaptability, I aim to transform complex datasets into meaningful insights that help organizations make better decisions.
